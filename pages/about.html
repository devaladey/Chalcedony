Our Story

Chalcedony School curriculum may best be described as a unique fusion of both Nigerian and British curricula. The school is divided into the High School (Day and Boarding), Junior School and Pre-School Sections. In the Junior School the students spend six years and take two ends- of -course examinations; The Lagos State Junior Secondary Certificate Examination (JSCE) and the Checkpoint (Cambridge) examination. The students in the high school spend five years and in their final year, the High School students take the following examinations: The Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE), the National Examination Council (NECO), and the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) (Cambridge) Examination.
